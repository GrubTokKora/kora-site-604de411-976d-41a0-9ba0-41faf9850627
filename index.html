A Dining Experience Like No Other

For over 20 years, Shiki Hibachi & Sushi has been Stamford's destination for unforgettable Japanese cuisine. Watch our skilled chefs perform the art of teppanyaki right before your eyes — flames, flair, and flavor all in one show. Our sushi bar serves the freshest fish, crafted into stunning rolls and traditional nigiri by our expert sushi chefs.
