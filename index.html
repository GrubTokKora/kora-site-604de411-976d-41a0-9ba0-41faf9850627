Experience Authentic Japanese Cuisine

Shiki Hibachi & Sushi is a premier Japanese restaurant in Stamford, specializing in interactive hibachi grill experiences, a diverse selection of fresh sushi and sashimi, and a variety of other authentic Japanese appetizers and entrees. We pride ourselves on using high-quality ingredients to deliver a memorable dining experience in our modern and welcoming atmosphere.
